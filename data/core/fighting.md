title: Fighting
description: An overview of how melee combat works

### Which models fight
The model selected to fight an all enemy models within engagement range of that model can fight.

### When models Fight
Start with the model with the highest initative, and then repeatedly fight with models until every model has fought. If 2 models have the same initiative, they fight simultaneously. To determine a models inititive select a melee weapon the model is equipped with, add the models initiative characteristic(I) to that weapons initiative modifier(IM), the result is the models initiative for this fight activation. That model must make its attacks with the weapon selected during this step.

### How models Fight
Before performing any attacks, the model first makes a pile in move of 3 inches were each model must end the move closer to the enemy model. If models are fighting simultaneously, the model that made a charge moves first. Otherwise roll off to determine who can move their models first.

Each model in engagement range can then make attacks with the melee weapon selected in [When Models Fight](#when-models-fight), which must be allocated to a model within engagement range of that model. See [Making Attacks](/0thEdition/core/makingattacks/) for what to do next.

After a model has fought, it can make a consolidation move of 3 inches closer to the nearest enemy model. If models are fighting simultaneously, the model that made a charge moves first. Otherwise roll off to determine who can move their models first.
