## Voice of Command
As a 1 AC action, IMPERIAL GUARD OFFICERS from your army can issue one the orders they know. The Commanding Authority section of each OFFICER's datasheet will specify which types of orders the officer knows, and how many orders it can issue per turn. 

In order to issue an order, roll 2d6; if the result is greater than or equal to the OFFICER's leadership characteristic the order is successful, otherwise the order fails and nothing happens. 

If issuing a Regimental Order, select one friendly INFANTRY unit within 6" of that OFFICER's unit. If issuing a Mechanised Order, select one friendly VEHICLE within 12" of that OFFICER's unit. A battleshocked unit or CHARACTER unit cannot be selected as the target of an order. 

The unit selected is then affected by that order. A unit can only be affect by one Order at a time.
 If a unit is being affected by an Order when it is selected to be affected by another Order, the new Order immediately replaces the old one. While a unit is battleshocked, the order has no affect until the unit is no longer battleshocked. 

## Regimental Tactics
Each time an OFFICER from your army issues a Regimental Order to a unit in your army with this ability, you may select one or more other friendly INFANTRY units within 6" of that unit that share this ability are are not battleshocked; those units are also affected by that Order. 

Each time an OFFICER from your army issues a Mechanised Order to a unit in your army with this ability, you may select one or more other friendly VEHICLE units within 6" of that unit that share this ability are are not battleshocked; those units are also affected by that Order. 

## Born Soldiers
Officers in your army have the following ability:
Born Soldiers(Aura):While a friendly Imperial Guard unit is within 6" of this model, models in that unit can use this OFFICER's cool characteristic when making battleshock tests. 

## Abilities

### Turret Weapons
If this model is within engagement range of an enemy unit, it can ignore the -1 to hit penalty with weapons with this ability so long as the target is not within engagement range of this unit. 

Each time an attack is made with this weapon, add 1 to that attacks hit roll. 

## Regimental Orders
### First Rank Fire Second Rank Fire
Add 1 to the attacks characteristic of HEAVY weapons equipped by models in this unit. 
### Take Aim!
Each time a model in this unit makes a ranged attack, add 1 to that attack's hit roll.
### Fix Bayonets!
Each time a model in this unit makes a melee attack, add 1 to that attack's hit roll.
### Take Cover!
Each time an attack is allocated to a model in this unit, improve the Cover Save of models in that unit by 1+. If that unit does not have a cover save it instead has a Cover Save of 6+
### Move! Move! Move!
Add 3 to the Move characteristic of models in this unit. 
### Show Them Steel, Show Them Contempt!
When making battleshock tests or cool tests for this unit, add 1 to that test. In addition, the unit has the Feel No Pain 5+ ability against mortal wounds. 

## Mechanised Orders
### Pound Them to Dust!
Whenever this unit is selected to shoot, for the purposes of determing how many attacks are made by Blast weapons, double the number of models in the target unit. 
### Full Throttle!
Add 2 to the Move characteristic of models in this unit. In addition, if this model made a Move action, it counts as having remained stationairy. 
### Gunners, Kill on Sight!
Each time this model makes an attack in overwatch, it may ignore the -1 to hit penalty. 
### Shock and Awe!
Add 3 to the OC characteristic of models in this unit
### Blitz Them!
Whenever this unit performs a charge action, add 1 to the charge roll. In addition this unit may be targeted with the Tank Shock Strategem for 0cp, even if another unit has already been targeted with that strategem this turn. 
### Pinning Fire!
Weapons equipped by this unit have the Pinning ability. 

## Tank Aces
When mustering your army, you can upgrade any IMPERIAL GUARD BATTLE TANK or IMPERIAL GUARD TITANIC models from your army by making them a Tank Ace. To make them a tank ace, select one of the skills presented below and increase the cost of that unit by the cost noted on the skill.

### Vaunted Praetorian-20pts
Officer Model Only. Reduce the AC cost of the first order the bearer issues each turn to 0. In addition, add 1 to the roll whenever the Officer attempts to issue an order. 
### Meticulous Calibrator-20pts
Each time this model makes a ranged attack, the weapon gains the Ignores Cover ability. 
### Mechanical Pack Rat-25pts
You may ignore the first roll on the critical damage table of each location on this unit. 
### Veteran Commandeer-20pts
Improve the Cool and Ballistic skill characteristic of models in this unit by 1. 
### Knight of Piety-35pts
This model has a 5+ invulnerable save, if this unit has the TITANIC keyword, it has a 6+ invulnerable save instead. 
In addition, each time this model would lose a wound as a result of a mortal wound, roll one D6: on a 5+ that wound is not lost. 
### Master of Camouflage-30pts
Each time a ranged attack targets this unit, if the attacker is more than 18" away, or 24" away if this model has the TITANIC keyword, this model cannot be selected as the target of those attacks.
### Steel Commissar-25pts
Officer model only. this model knows Regimental orders in addition to Mechanised Orders. In addition, when issuing a Regimental order, it may select INFANTRY units within 12" instead of 6", and reduce the AC cost of the first Regimental order to 0. 

## Warlord Traits
### Front Line Combatant
Each time this WARLORD makes a melee attack:
- An unmodified hit roll of 6 scores 2 additional hits.
- Add 1 to that attack’s wound roll.
### Master Tactician
At the start of the first battle round, you can select up to three friendly ASTRA MILITARUM units (excluding TITANIC units). Remove those units from the battlefield, then set them up anywhere on the battlefield that is wholly within your deployment zone. If the mission you are playing uses the Strategic Reserves rules, you can place any of those units into Strategic Reserves instead.
### Grand Strategist
While this WARLORD is on the battlefield, each time you spend a Command point to use a Stratagem, roll one D6: on a 5+, that Command point is refunded.
### Superior Tactical Training
When you select this Warlord Trait, select one type of Orders that this WARLORD does not know. This WARLORD knows those Orders in addition to any others it knows. 
### Old Grudges
At the start of the first battle round, select one enemy unit. Until the end of the battle, this WARLORD gains the following ability:

Old Grudges(Aura):While a friendly IMPERIAL GUARD unit is within 6" of this WARLORD, each time a model in that friendly unit makes an attack against that enemy unit, add 1 to the wound roll or armour penetration tests. 
### Lead By Example
This WARLORD can issue Orders to itself, even though you cannot normally slect CHARACTER units when issuing Orders.

## Stratagems
### Fields of Fire 1CP
When: Activation Phase
Target: Two IMPERIAL GUARD INFANTRY units that have not activated. 
Effect:Select one enemy unit visible to both units. Until the end of the phase, when targeting that unit, weapons equipped by both units have the Pinning ability.
### Reinforcements! 2CP
When: Any Phase
Target: One BATTLELINE unit from your army that was just destroyed. You can target that unit with the Stratagem even though it was destroyed. 
Effect: Add a new unit to your army identitcal to your destroyed unit, in Strategic Reserves, at its Starting Strength and with all its wounds remaining. 
### Crash Through 1CP
When: Activation Phase
Target: One IMPERIAL GUARD VEHICLE unit from your army that has just been activated. 
Effect:Until the end of the activation, each time your unit makes a Move, Run, or Charge action it can move horizontally through terrain features as if they were not there. 
### On my Position! 1CP
When: Activation Phase, just after an enemy unit makes a fight action  \
Target: One INFANTRY unit from your army within Engagement range of that enemy unit \
Effect: Roll one D6 for each enemy unit within Engagement Range of your unit; on a 2+ that enemy unit suffers 2D3 mortal wounds. Then your unit suffers 4D3 Mortal wounds \

## Detachments

### Combined Arms

#### Detachment Rule
Each time an IMPERIAL GUARD unit from your army makes a ranged attack that targets a visible unit, that attack has the Rending 6+ ability

#### Warlord Trait-Reactive Commander
OFFICER model only. The first time an enemy unit ends a move within 9" of the bearer, the bearer can issue one order at no AC cost. This order is not counted towards the number of Order the bearer can issue each turn. 

#### Relic - Relic of Lost Cadia
CADIAN model only. Once per battle, in any Command phase, the bearer can use this Relic. If it does so, until the end of the turn, the bearer gains the following ability:

Relic of Lost Cadia (Aura): While a friendly CADIAN INFANTRY unit is within 6" of this model, improve the Weapon Skill, Ballistic Skill and Leadership characteristics of models in that unit by 1 and add 1 to the Attack characteristic of melee weapons in that unit.
