## What is Warhammer 0th Edition?
Warhammer 0th Edition our solution to the many problems we have with modern 40k/10th edition.
Aiming to address the issues that result from the "you go I go system" and the lack of flavor
and variety present in 10th edition. This homebrew edition combines many ideas from various Warhammer games, such as the alternating activation system of Necromunda, the Vehicle Armor System of Horus Heresy, and the simplified list/army building of 10th(with additional flavor and options). 
:::note
Currently the project is in early development, with the core rules still being ironed out and refined. If you have any feedback or ideas be sure to let us know!
:::
:::caution
Warhammer 0th Edition is a free and open source project. This project exists soley to provide a custom way of playing Warhammer 40k and does not exist to replace or profit off of existing Warhammer games or any other properties by Games Workshop.
